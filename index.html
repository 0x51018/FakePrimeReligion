History

The Prime Faith Fellowship began in the early days of the internet, when a small group of enthusiasts discovered the divine beauty of prime numbers. Inspired by the mysteries of mathematics, they formed a community dedicated to celebrating these indivisible jewels.

Our digital sanctuary pays homage to the simple, yet profound, elegance of primes. We gather to appreciate the unique qualities that set them apart from all other numbers.

Beliefs

We believe that prime numbers represent purity and perseverance. Each prime stands alone, resolute against division by any other number. In this strength, we find a metaphor for personal integrity and truth.

Members of the Fellowship celebrate primes in all aspects of life, finding comfort in sequences such as 2, 3, 5, 7, and beyond. Through playful rituals like the Prime Chant and Factorization Feast, we honor these special numbers.

Join Us

If you're drawn to the mystique of primes, we invite you to join our community. Share your favorite prime, create art inspired by numbers, or simply bask in the glow of indivisibility.

Our gatherings are open to all who appreciate the whimsical side of mathematics.
